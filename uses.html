Uses of Transposable Elements

Gene Editing

DNA transposons can be inserted into a genome as foreign DNA and from there, changes in behavior can be analyzed in order to better understand gene development and disease growth

Type of transposon that can be inserted into the genome and to see which areas are responsible for cancers through mutagenesis in tumors and other areas of tissue

Type of transposon that is able to be used for gene trapping experiments in tissue culture cells

Type of transposon that is used primarily in insects, are able to alter the diseases that they are carrying in order to reduce sickness rates
